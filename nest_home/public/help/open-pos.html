Open POS (Point of Sale)

NestERP Help

What This Does

This tile lets you open the Point of Sale interface to process retail sales.

Steps

1 Click Open POS from your home screen
2 Select your name from the Select POS User dropdown
3 The POS interface loads with your store's inventory
4 Search or scan items to add them to the cart
5 Select the payment method and complete the sale
If you need help with a specific field, hover over its label to see a tooltip description.
